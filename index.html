How it works

Step 1 — Smart Parse: Upload the BOQ (.xlsx/.xls/.csv). The SmartBOQParser reads ALL raw rows as one block, reconstructs fragmented descriptions (rows without item numbers are merged into the previous item), filters out Bill headers, section labels (e.g. "Kitchen Area", "Bathroom"), RFQ headers, totals, and compliance notes. Only genuine line items survive.

Step 2 — Trade Classification: Each item is classified into a construction trade: Carpentry, Plumbing, Painting, Aluminum/Glazing, Fencing, Tiling, Electrical, Steelwork, Roofing, Civil/Earthworks, Flooring, or General Building. The classifier uses construction-specific keyword scoring with negative keywords to prevent misclassification.

Step 3 — Google Search: For each line item, the system calls Serper API (Google ZA) to find 7+ live South African supplier URLs.

Step 4 — Scrape & Extract: Each page is fetched directly or via FlareSolverr (Cloudflare bypass). Prices are extracted using 4 strategies: JSON-LD, meta tags, JSON blobs, and ZAR regex.

Step 5 — Labour Costing: Installation & labour rates from MBSA, BIBC, CETA & Dept of Labour 2024/2025 guidelines.

Step 6 — Report: Full competitive tender report with BOQ Overview, Supplier Prices, Best-Price Quote, Installation & Labour, Measurements & Units, and SA Compliance.

Step 7 — Export: Download the full report as PDF or Word document (.docx), or export as CSV.

✅ API keys are hardcoded for static deployment. No server or environment variables needed. Serper & FlareSolverr are called directly from your browser.
